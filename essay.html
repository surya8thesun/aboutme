Pandemic Haircut

It had been a long time. The coronavirus pandemic had spread rapidly and the lockdown had increased. But the lockdown was not the only thing that extended. My hair had also extended exponentially. Since the odds of finding a barber were down to none, we had to deal with it ourselves , which we did, today.
Now you see, I had a specific routine , one I'd rather not skip. It is crucial. One thing out of balance and my day is done with and everything goes all over the place.
My schedule is pretty simple. I wake up at arond nine o'clock, brush my teeth, study just a little , bathe and then code for pretty much the whole day .I spend my whole summer like this. Now, when I spend an hour at something that is not in my routine, like a haircut, then I feel troubled for I could have done something productive.
Well with such a schedule, getting a haircut is a lot of trouble . Such trouble I felt today.
I woke up ( at 9:00 AM ) and immediately was taken to the hall. Just when things felt normal, my dad took up a scissor and started cutting my hair, and I was not the happiest.
I don't have a problem with haircuts. But with a schedule like mine,and the duration of a haircut, you are in the red zone.
Anyway, I was getting my haircut,and time was going fast. Anxiety was building up. Due to all this, I could not sit still, which is like most of the haircut.
Finally,at 10:05 ( too late ) , my dad was done and I was done with. Afraid of how I would look ( happens always ) I looked into the mirror.
Surprise! What I saw was awesome! It looked pretty decent. It was plain and good looking at the same time ( which is hard ).
It also so happened that after a little mending, I was able to get back on track with my routine.
So, all-in-all, it was a pretty good day ( or morning ) .
